## Key Risk: Transition Failure (CR-01)

This is the highest consequence risk in the procurement. A failed transition would cause service disruption across 47 buildings, potential WHS incidents, and significant reputational damage. Treatment plan:

1. Mobilisation period of 10 weeks built into contract program
2. Transition plan (covering all 47 buildings) required as a contract deliverable within 4 weeks of execution
3. Site handover inspections with condition reports for all buildings
4. IT system transition (CAFM platform) with parallel running for 4 weeks minimum
5. Staff TUPE (transfer of employment) obligations managed with HR
6. Step-in rights included -- organisation can mobilise a third party at contractor cost if transition fails
7. Current incumbent (Ventia) contractually required to cooperate with successor contractor during transition under the expiring contract's novation provisions

**Contingency:** If transition is assessed as high risk at the 8-week mark before commencement (by 5 August 2026), the organisation will exercise a short extension of the current contract (30-90 days) to allow completion of transition without service disruption.
