## Scope of Engagement

Meridian will deliver the following services:

**Phase 1 -- Current State Assessment (February -- March 2026)**
- Review of current ERM framework document, risk appetite statement, risk register, and ARC reporting
- Structured interviews with 12 business unit risk champions
- Benchmark assessment against ISO 31000:2018 and QAO better practice standards
- Deliverable: Current State Assessment Report

**Phase 2 -- Framework Development (April -- May 2026)**
- Gap analysis against ISO 31000:2018
- Development of updated ERM framework document
- Development of updated risk appetite statement
- Development of revised ARC reporting template
- Development of risk management capability uplift program
- Deliverables: Draft ERM framework, draft risk appetite statement, revised ARC template, capability program design

**Phase 3 -- Consultation and Finalisation (May -- June 2026)**
- Facilitation of business unit consultation sessions (up to 3 group sessions)
- Facilitation of ARC workshop on draft framework and risk appetite statement
- Incorporation of feedback and finalisation of all documents
- Deliverables: Final ERM framework, final risk appetite statement, final ARC template

**Phase 4 -- Implementation Support (June -- July 2026)**
- Delivery of risk champion training program (up to 4 sessions)
- Preparation of ARC endorsement paper
- Deliverables: Training materials, ARC paper
