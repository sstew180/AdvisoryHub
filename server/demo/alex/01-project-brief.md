# Enterprise Risk Framework Review — Project Brief

**Project title:** Enterprise Risk Framework Review
**Project sponsor:** Chief Executive Officer
**Project manager:** Alex Morgan, Manager Risk and Insurance
**Date:** February 2026
**Version:** 1.2 (approved)

---

## Purpose

This project will deliver a reviewed and updated Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) Framework for the organisation, replacing the current framework which was last substantively updated in 2019. The review has been initiated in response to:

1. QAO management letter findings from the 2024-25 financial audit (three findings rated Significant relating to risk management)
2. The Audit and Risk Committee's request (October 2025) for a comprehensive framework review
3. Material changes to the operating environment including post-COVID risk profile changes, digital transformation risks, and the approaching 2032 Olympic Games infrastructure program

---

## Objectives

1. Assess the current ERM framework against ISO 31000:2018 and QAO better practice expectations
2. Identify gaps between current practice and better practice
3. Develop an updated framework including revised risk appetite statement, risk assessment methodology, and risk reporting templates
4. Embed the updated framework across all 12 business units by 30 June 2026
5. Present the updated framework to the ARC for endorsement by August 2026

---

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Review of the current ERM framework document (version 4.1, June 2019)
- Review of the current risk appetite statement (last updated March 2021)
- Review of risk reporting to the ARC and Executive Leadership Team
- Review of risk management practices across all 12 business units via structured interviews
- Development of updated framework, risk appetite statement, and reporting templates
- Development and delivery of a risk management capability uplift program for business unit risk champions

**Out of scope:**
- Review of project-level risk registers (addressed separately under the Project Management Framework review)
- Insurance program review (separate project, Q3 2026)
- Information security risk framework (addressed under the Cybersecurity Strategy)
